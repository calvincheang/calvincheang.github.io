Born and raised in Brooklyn NY, I am a creative professional with a Bachelor’s degree in Integrated Digital Media from New York University (NYU) Tandon School of Engineering.

I currently am at my dream job at the Metropolitan Transportation Authority (MTA) as a Digital Content and Multimedia Manager for their Internal Communications department. Every day I get to work with a lovely team to create and manage content for over 75,000 employees, providing them with the information they need to know, while elevating the employee experience with fun initiatives, videos, and more.

Outside of my professional work, I draw inspiration from many of my personal interests, including food, rail transit systems, and anime. These passions continue to influence my creativity, perspective, and appreciation for visual storytelling and immersive experiences.
